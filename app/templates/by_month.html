Bicycle Accidents by Month Over Each Year

It's useful to see the number of accidents over the course of each month by year. We can observe the seasonality per year and also how the frequency of accidents change over the course of the year. It's easy to see that the most dangerous months are in the summer, which makes sense considering that ridership is at the highest during that time of the year. You can also see as the years pass, that the number of accidents overall appear to increase.
